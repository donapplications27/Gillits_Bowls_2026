The Mist-Belt Advantage.

Winston Park's unique micro-climate provides a technical edge. Our greens remain supple and vibrant, protected by the soft Atlantic-to-Indian mists.

While other clubs swelter, Gillitts offers a refreshing, high-performance environment that allows for longer play sessions and sharper mental focus.
