Game Innovation

B20 "Cricket-Bowls"

Fast-paced, tactical, and high-energy. Pioneered here in 2016 by Malcolm Webster and Andy van den Heuvel, B20 brings the thrill of limited-overs cricket to the green.

Our Edge

The Mist-Belt
Advantage.

Winston Park's unique micro-climate provides a technical edge. Our greens remain supple and vibrant, protected by the soft Atlantic-to-Indian mists.

While other clubs swelter, Gillitts offers a refreshing, high-performance environment for longer play sessions and sharper mental focus.
